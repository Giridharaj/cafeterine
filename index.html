Giving you an awesome experience to enjoy our amenities, dining etc.,Our story began in 1971 along the cobblestone streets of Seattle’s historic Pike Place Market. From a single store, Starbucks set out to be a different kind of company—one that celebrated coffee and the rich tradition, while bringing a feeling of connection. Inspired by Italian coffeehouse culture, we envisioned a "third place" between work and home—a welcoming space to meet friends, work, or simply enjoy a moment to yourself. Today, with thousands of stores across the globe, we are committed to nurturing the limitless possibilities of human connection with every cup, every conversation, and every community.
